Here are some of the things I have done.

Whether it be building a mechanized beaver or completely taking apart a computer, the first semester at Olin has involved many projects.

IT Technician

I work at Olin's IT Help Desk, maintaining the infrastructure of the college for both students and faculty.

Modelling the Real World

For one of my ModSim projects, I modeled the rate of dialysis using real world data.

Designing a Beaver

When I think of a kids toy, I don't think of a beaver. However, for DesNat, my group built and tested a mechanized swimming beaver that could be controlled remotely.

CAD and Beaver

To build a beaver, you must design the beaver first. Though I am interested more on the software side of things, I helped the group come up with the skeleton and mountings for the servos.

ModCon Projects

ModCon was the standard introduction to circuits course, with a final project. I worked with one other student to build a line following robot using photodiodes and little else.

Creativity among Engineers

I also keep practicing Piano, something that has been a part of my life for 14 years now. This year, I was able to take The Wired Ensemble, where I composed two new pieces bi-weekly for an audience.
